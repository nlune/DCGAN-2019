Click on Random Image to get a random face to fill, 
or select your own face image (must be RGB image cropped to 64x64).

In the Settings, you can choose the number of iterations to backpropagate the error. 
The original paper did 1000, but if you're running on CPU we recommend 100 or less. 
You can choose to use the weighted mask for the importance weighted context loss. 
You can also save the mask and masked images in the chosen folder. 

Choose a mask and click Start Inpaint to get started! 

Click Save Image button after to save the image(s) to the folder you selected.
